Welcome Home

The Local Community Event Portal is designed to bring our residents closer together by providing easy access to community events, services, and resources.

  • Browse upcoming community events
  • Register for events of your interest
  • Check event locations and directions
  • Access basic city services
  • Connect with your community

Tip: Use the navigation menu above to explore Events and Contact sections, or visit our Help page for more information.

About Our Portal

This lightweight, browser-based portal was created to make it easy for residents to stay connected with their community and never miss out on important local events.

Whether you're looking to volunteer, attend social gatherings, or learn about community initiatives, this portal is your one-stop destination.
