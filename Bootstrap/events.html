About Our Events

Our community events are open to all residents. Some events are free while others may have a small fee. Registration helps us plan better for attendance.

Theater Night, Cleanup Drive, and Book Club are completely free to attend. Just register and show up!

The Potluck Festival has a $5 suggested donation and the Fun Run has a $15 registration fee that supports youth programs.

The Cleanup Drive is a volunteer event. Participants receive community service certificates upon completion.
