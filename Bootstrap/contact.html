Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for an event?

Visit the Events page and click the "Register Now" button for any event you're interested in.

Can I cancel my registration?

Yes, you can manage your registrations from your account dashboard.

Are events free?

Most events are free, but some may have a nominal fee. Check the event details for pricing.

How will I receive event updates?

Subscribe to notifications on the Events page to get updates delivered to your email.
