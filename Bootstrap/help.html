Getting Started

New to our portal? Here's what you need to know:

  1. Browse the Events page
  2. Click "Register Now" on any event
  3. Fill out your details
  4. Check your email for confirmation
Finding Events

Our Events page shows all upcoming events with:

  • Event name and description
  • Date and time
  • Location
  • Registration button
Registration

Registering is simple and takes just a few minutes:

  1. Find the event you want
  2. Click "Register Now"
  3. Provide your contact info
  4. Confirm your registration
